Euler Golf 2

Use the left and right arrow keys as navigation & hover over the bottom right corner for controls.

Rules

  • Every move consists of a 90 degree rotation around your last position.
  • You begin at the point one unit right from the center.
  • The inital point that you rotate around is the origin (blue).
  • You must navigate to the target point (white).

Initial game by speleo, reimplemented & solved by simponic.
